What We Do

Hardware Supply Division

Our hardware supply division offers a wide range of products, including servers, workstations, laptops, networking equipment, and storage solutions. We partner with some of the world's top hardware manufacturers to ensure that our clients have access to the latest and most advanced technology. We also offer installation, configuration, and maintenance services to ensure that your hardware is always running smoothly.

We work with businesses of all sizes, from small startups to large corporations, and we are committed to providing the same high level of service to each and every one of our clients.

In addition to our wide range of hardware products, we also offer financing options to help businesses manage their IT expenses and stay within budget.

We understand that downtime can be costly for businesses, which is why we offer 24/7 support to our clients to ensure that any hardware issues are resolved as quickly as possible.

Our installation and configuration services are designed to be as efficient as possible, so that businesses can get up and running with their new hardware as soon as possible.

At Flying Data Developers, we are passionate about technology, and we are constantly exploring new hardware solutions to ensure that our clients have access to the best and most innovative products on the market.

Professional Services

Our service division offers a variety of support options to help businesses maintain their hardware and software infrastructure. Our team of experts is available 24/7 to assist with troubleshooting, repairs, and upgrades. We also offer managed services, including backup and disaster recovery, network security, and cloud solutions.

We provide tailored support packages to meet the unique needs of each of our clients, so whether you need ongoing maintenance, emergency support, or one-off assistance, we have a service plan that will work for you.

We offer proactive monitoring and maintenance services to help prevent issues before they arise, which can save businesses time and money in the long run.

Our managed services solutions are designed to help businesses optimize their technology infrastructure, so that they can focus on their core operations and leave the technology to us.

We are committed to providing excellent customer service throughout the entire process, from initial consultation to ongoing support, and we pride ourselves on building long-lasting relationships with our clients.
